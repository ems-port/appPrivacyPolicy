# Cubie Solver Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Cubie Solver is designed to work primarily on your device.

## Information We Collect

Cubie Solver does not require account creation.

Cubie Solver does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number for app operation.

The app may process the following on your device:

- Camera input used to scan a 3x3 cube
- App settings and preferences
- Local leaderboard entries you choose to save
- Solve credit balance stored locally on your device

## How Information Is Used

This information is used only to provide the app's features, including:

- scanning cube faces
- generating solve steps
- saving local preferences and scores
- tracking locally stored solve credits

## Data Sharing

Cubie Solver does not sell your personal data.

Cubie Solver does not use advertising SDKs.

Cubie Solver does not share your data with third parties for marketing.

If you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your payment card details.

## Data Storage

App data is primarily stored locally on your device using on-device storage.

If you contact support by email, the information you include in that email will be received by us so we can respond.

## Camera Access

Cubie Solver requests camera permission only so the app can scan your cube.

## Children's Privacy

Cubie Solver does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your Choices

You can stop using the app at any time.

You can delete locally stored app data by removing the app from your device or using any in-app reset options that are available.

## Contact

If you have privacy questions about Cubie Solver, contact:

`privacy@cubiesolver.app`
